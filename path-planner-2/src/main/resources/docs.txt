M - Mirror across centre
I - Invert direction
D - Delete Point
N - New Point
T - Insert Quick Turn
R - Add Reverse Path
Space - Begin Simulation
Arrow Key - Translate Selected Point
Q, W - Rotate Selected Point by 15 degrees
Shift + Q, W - Rotate Selected Point by 1 degree
Minus, Equal - Change Max Velocity Limit by 10%
Brackets - Change Max Acceleration Limit by 10%
J - Enable/Disable Jerk Limiting
G - Show Graphs
O - Enable/Disable Curvature Change Optimization

In Simulation:
Space - Pause
Escape - Stop
Minus, Equal - Shift by 0.1 seconds
Brackets - Shift by 1 second
